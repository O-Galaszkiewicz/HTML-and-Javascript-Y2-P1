Rules of Hazard:

Place Your Bet:

Enter your desired bet amount and choose a main number - 5 to 9.

Rolling the Dice:

Press the "Roll" button to roll two dice.

Check Your Roll:

Lose Conditions:

Roll a dice total of 2 or 3.

Roll an 11 or 12 if your main is 5 or 9.

Roll 11 if your main is 6 or 8.

Roll 12 if your main is 7.

Win Conditions:

Roll a total that matches your main.

Roll 12 if your main is 6 or 8.

Roll 11 if your main is 7.

If your roll matches your chosen "Chance" after an initial roll, you win.

After the Roll:

If you lose, your bet is gone.

If you win, your bet is doubled.

If you roll a "Chance", you can continue rolling until you either win or lose based on your chosen main number.
