Rules of Blackjack:

Place Your Bet:

Enter your desired bet amount.

Hitting Cards:

Press the "Hit" button to add more cards to your deck.

Check Your Card Total:

Lose Conditions:

You have a Card Total exceeding 21.

The Dealers Card Total is more than yours.

Draw Condition:

Both you and the Dealer have the same Card Totals.

Win Conditions:

You have more than the Dealers Card Total.

The Dealer busts.
